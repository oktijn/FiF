introduction

the bible of FiF is flamboyant and requires a dictionary to read. This page is meant to explain every word that causes mild confusion while reading the lore of FiF.

Sumptis Pedibus

the Sumptis Pedibus, a.k.a. "fuck it feet gif", is a videographic sequence of motion pictures describing a short story with deep meanings behind it. It was found on a relic, owned by one of 2 Pedes, on the 29th of november, 2022.

story has been told about the gif encompassing the secret behind immortality and ultimate ecstacy, though these theories are unconfirmed.

Lethargus

Lethargus is a city in the Promised land, Yakusoku. it is renowned for being both the biggest city and the only city with people who believe in Fifism. it has a population of around 250 000 people and it looks futuristic.

Pedes

Pedes (singular: Pedites) means "Gods" in Latin, the native language of Lethargus. every Pedes is alive and well, living in Lethargus. because the city is heavily religious, the Pedites called Veneratio is the mayor. Everyone (except for atheists and agnostic people) pray and worship these 2 Pedes.
